   Newotons Second Law was
   \begin{equation}\label{a}
      F=ma
   \end{equation}

   $$
   F_{net} = m \cdot \frac{d^2 y}{d x^2} 
   $$
   Or as

   \[
   \sum F = ma
   \]

   for an oscilating object the restoring force is : $F = -kx$ \\ 
   so the net force 
   \begin{align*}
      F &= ma\\
      &= -kx
